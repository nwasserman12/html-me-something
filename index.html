Bradenton, FL

I was born in Bradenton, FL on August 23, 1995. Bradenton is roughly 56 minutes south of Tampa, Fl. Now I don't remember much of anything about Bradenton as I was just a wee baby during this time. I am told it is a cool little costal city of 56k people. I lived with my mom and dad for a period of time before getting adopted by my grandparents and moving down to Pinecrest to live with them.

Pinecrest, FL

My sister and I came to live with my Grandparents in Pinecrest when we were pretty young, she was around 2, I was around 1. Pinecrest was a great neighborhood to grow up in! It is a suburb of Miami, so there was plenty of things to do. Life was never boring.
